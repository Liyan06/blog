Therefore, given $x$, the label predicted by the model is $$ \hat{y}=\underset{y}{\operatorname{argmax}} p(y | x ; w)=\underset{y}{\operatorname{argmax}} \sum_{j=1}^J w_{j} F_{j}(x, y) $$...
